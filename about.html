About AIVideoToolReview

Last updated: May 2026

What this site is

AIVideoToolReview is an independent review site covering AI SaaS tools — primarily AI video generation, marketing automation, and developer tooling. Every review on this site is based on hands-on testing of the actual product, not press releases or feature lists copied from vendor sites.

Who runs it

The site is operated by Huy Tran, a senior performance marketer with 12+ years in digital marketing in Vietnam — previously at VNG (Vietnam's largest tech & gaming company) and an AI recruitment startup, currently leading paid acquisition at a fintech firm managing 7-figure monthly ad budgets. AIVideoToolReview is an independent side project — there is no editorial team, no sponsored content desk, and no third-party investors.

How reviews are written

Each tool is tested through its real signup flow with a fresh email and credit card (where required). We score on four dimensions: ease of use, output quality, pricing fairness, and value for SMB buyers. We do not accept payment in exchange for higher rankings or favorable wording — vendors have no input on how their product is reviewed.

Affiliate disclosure

This site contains affiliate links. When you click through to a tool and subscribe, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. Affiliate relationships do not influence which products are featured or how they are ranked. We disclose affiliate links inline and in our Privacy Policy. If a tool fails our independent testing, we say so — even if it pays the highest commission.

What we do not do

  • We do not publish sponsored articles or paid placements.
  • We do not sell user data — see Privacy Policy.
  • We do not promote tools we have not personally tested.
  • We do not bid on branded keywords of the tools we review (per most affiliate program terms).
