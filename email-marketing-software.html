Detailed Reviews

Brevo (formerly Sendinblue)

Brevo has evolved from a transactional email service into a full-scale marketing platform. It is our top pick for 2026 because of its unique pricing model—you pay based on the number of emails you send, not the number of contacts you have. This makes it incredibly cost-effective for businesses with large lists that don't send daily blasts.

Standout features include a built-in CRM, SMS marketing, and one of the most reliable transactional email engines in the industry. For small businesses, the free plan is unbeatable as it allows for unlimited contacts.

Pros

  • Best free tier in the market
  • Unlimited contacts on all plans
  • Strong deliverability rates
  • Includes SMS and CRM features

Cons

  • Daily send cap on free plan
  • Interface can feel slightly dated

Pricing: Free (300 emails/day), Starter $9/mo, Business $18/mo

ActiveCampaign

ActiveCampaign is the undisputed leader when it comes to marketing automation. It's built for users who want to create complex, personalized customer journeys. Their visual workflow builder is best-in-class, allowing you to trigger emails based on site tracking, link clicks, and even physical location.

While it lacks a permanent free plan, the 14-day trial is enough to see the power of its 870+ integrations and integrated CRM. It’s the platform of choice for agencies and ecommerce businesses that need more than just simple newsletters.

Pros

  • Most powerful automation engine
  • Fully integrated sales CRM
  • 870+ third-party integrations
  • Excellent customer support

Cons

  • Steeper learning curve for beginners
  • No permanent free plan

Pricing: Starts $15/mo (Starter, 1,000 contacts), no free plan

GetResponse

GetResponse is more than just email; it's an all-in-one marketing ecosystem. Beyond standard email campaigns, it offers a robust landing page builder, webinar hosting, and paid ad management within a single dashboard. This makes it ideal for marketers who want to run entire funnel campaigns from one tool.

Their "Conversion Funnels" feature is a standout, simplifying the process of building lead magnets and sales pages. For those focused on list growth, their webinar feature is a powerful lead generation tool that many competitors lack.

Pros

  • Webinar hosting built-in
  • Advanced landing page builder
  • Excellent deliverability
  • 24/7 live chat support

Cons

  • Interface can feel crowded with features
  • Webinar feature requires higher tier plans

Pricing: Free (500 contacts), Email Marketing $15.58/mo

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best email marketing software for small business?
For most small businesses, Brevo is the best choice due to its value and unlimited contact limit. If you have complex needs and a higher budget, ActiveCampaign is superior for its automation capabilities.
Is there a free email marketing tool?
Yes, many tools offer free plans. Brevo offers the best free tier (unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day). GetResponse and Mailchimp also offer free plans capped at 500 contacts.
How much does email marketing software cost?
Prices range from $0 to $25/mo for most small businesses starting out. Costs typically scale based on your subscriber count or the volume of emails sent per month.
Brevo vs ActiveCampaign — which is better?
Choose Brevo if you want simplicity, value, and a generous free plan. Choose ActiveCampaign if you need advanced automation, a sales CRM, and have the time to learn a more complex platform.
